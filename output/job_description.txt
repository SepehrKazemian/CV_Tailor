Job Title: Senior Machine Learning Engineer

Company: TechInnovate AI

Location: Remote (US-based)

About Us:
TechInnovate AI is a leading AI solutions provider specializing in developing cutting-edge machine learning applications for enterprise clients. We're looking for a talented Senior Machine Learning Engineer to join our growing team.

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Machine Learning Engineer to design, develop, and deploy production-grade ML systems. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in machine learning, deep learning, and software engineering, with experience building and deploying models at scale.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement machine learning models and algorithms to solve complex business problems
- Build and maintain scalable ML pipelines for training, evaluation, and deployment
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand requirements and translate them into technical solutions
- Optimize existing ML systems for performance, scalability, and reliability
- Implement best practices for ML/MLOps, including monitoring, testing, and documentation
- Mentor junior engineers and contribute to the team's technical growth
- Stay current with the latest research and technologies in ML/AI

Requirements:
- 5+ years of experience in machine learning engineering or related field
- Strong programming skills in Python and experience with ML frameworks (PyTorch or TensorFlow)
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS preferred) and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Knowledge of data processing frameworks (Spark, Pandas) and databases (SQL, NoSQL)
- Experience with MLOps tools and practices (MLflow, Kubeflow, or similar)
- Familiarity with NLP and LLM technologies is a plus
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
- Excellent communication and collaboration abilities
- BS/MS/PhD in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or related field

Nice to Have:
- Experience with LangChain, LlamaIndex, or similar LLM frameworks
- Knowledge of vector databases and RAG systems
- Experience with real-time ML systems and streaming data
- Contributions to open-source ML projects
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices

- Flexible work schedule and remote-first culture
- Professional development budget

- 401(k) matching
